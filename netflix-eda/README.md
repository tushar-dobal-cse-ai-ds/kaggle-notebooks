# Netflix Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

This project performs exploratory data analysis on the Netflix Movies and TV Shows dataset
to uncover insights related to content distribution, growth trends, genres, and audience focus.

## Overview
- Dataset cleaning and preprocessing
- Feature engineering (year added, duration)
- Movies vs TV Shows analysis
- Genre and duration distribution
- Country-wise content analysis

## Key Insights
- Netflix content grew rapidly after 2015.
- Movies dominate the platform, though TV shows are increasing.
- Most movies are 80â€“120 minutes long.
- Dramas and Comedies are the most common genres.
- The US and India are top content producers.
